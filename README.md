Gram Route
07/2025
GramRoute is an innovative civic engagement
platform that empowers students and young travelers
to become active reporters of real-world problems in
rural and semi-urban India, where over 65% of the
population lives but often goes unheard due to weak
reporting systems. Unlike existing grievance apps that
rely only on local residents, GramRoute transforms
everyday student journeys into opportunities for civic
participation by allowing users to capture photos or
videos of issues such as garbage, potholes, water leaks,
broken streetlights, or unsafe public places,
automatically tagged with GPS location and time.
These reports are filtered through AI checks and
human moderation to ensure authenticity before
being routed to the correct Gram Panchayat,
municipal authority, or nodal officer. Students can
then track the resolution status in real time—whether
the issue has been received, seen, is in progress, or
resolved—closing the gap between citizens and
authorities.
